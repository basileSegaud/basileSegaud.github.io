Basilica of Fourviere

The Basilica of Notre-Dame de Fourvière (French: Basilique Notre-Dame de Fourvière) is a basilica in Lyon. It was built with private funds between 1872 and 1884 in a dominant position overlooking the city..

The site it occupies was once the Roman forum of Trajan, the forum vetus (old forum), thus its name (as an inverted corruption of the French Vieux-Forum)
